Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 hoagie rolls
  • 1 can beef consomme
  • 1 cup water
  • thinly sliced daily roast beef 1 pound
  • 8 slices cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Open hoagie rolls and lay them on a baking sheet.
  3. Heat beef consomme and water in a pan to make beef broth.
  4. Place roasted beef in broth and cook for 3 minutes.
  5. Put the roasted beef on the rolls and serve with French dip.
